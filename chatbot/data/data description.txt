Financial Dataset Description:

This dataset contains Australian Government Social Services budget data covering:

1. Income Statement (3.1): Revenue, expenses, and net income
2. Balance Sheet (3.2): Assets, liabilities, and equity
3. Changes in Equity (3.3): Equity movements over time
4. Cash Flow Statement (3.4): Operating, investing, and financing activities

Time Period: 2024-25, 2025-26, 2026-27 financial years
Key Agencies: DSS, NDIA, NDIS, SAUS, AFIS, DFSV
